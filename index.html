LOPOTA TOUR

Enjoy the unexplored nature of Kakheti with one of our tours

Air, Helicopter & Balloon Tours

Escape your daily routine and fly away to Tusheti Protected Areas. Air balloon and light aircraft tours help you enjoy spectacular landscapes and savor the wonderful views of Alazani Valley.

Eco tours

Ever wondered where the magic comes from? Arrange your trip and visit the natural areas of Kakheti.

Jeep tours

Discover the unique sights of Vashlovani Protected Area - the desert, steppe and forest zones have existed together on a tiny, wonderful piece of land.

Wine tours in Kakheti

There is something about Georgian wine that makes people desperately thirsty for more. Don’t miss an opportunity to try new wines and to discover grapes that have that ideal balance of lightness and warmth. You can enjoy the following Georgian wines: Khareba Winery, Kindzmarauli Corporation, Telavi Wine Cellar, Teliani Valley, Shumi, Chateau Mere, Schuchmann Wines Chateau and Twins Wine Cellar.

Wine tastings at family cellars

Now it is time to discover the new tastes of Georgian wines. Arrange wine tours at family cellars. Visit Papa Zurabo’s Wine Cellar, Velistsikhe Museum-Cellar, Pheasant’s Tears and Dimitri’s Wine House.

Bodbe Monastery

Visit a Georgian orthodox monastic complex where St. Nino’s relics are shrined.

Nekresi Monastery

Nekresi monastery is one of the largest monastery complexes in Kakheti. Visit a place which was founded by St. Abibos Nekreseli, one of the famous thirteen Assyrian fathers.

Alaverdi Monastery

Alaverdi Monastery is a Georgian Eastern Orthodox monastery dating to the 6th century and is definitely a must-see.

David Gareja Monastery Complex

The complex attracts many visitors all over the world. It includes hundreds of cells, churches, chapels and living quarters carefully engineered out of solid rocks.

Gremi Monastery

Gremi is a 16th century architectural monument and a great complex. Located on a hill, the complex is known for its amazing nature.

Ikalto Monastery

Throughout the centuries Ikalto Monastery has been known as the most significant cultural center of Kakheti.

Shuamta Monastery

Visit Shuamta Monastery surrounded by beautiful hills and prepare to get impressed.

Telavi

Imagine the beautiful city full of art museums, castles and world-famous wineries. Discover Telavi.

Kvareli

Wine lovers, great travellers and history connoisseurs will all find something for themselves here. Visit now! The pretty town is waiting for you.

Signagi

It is almost impossible not to fall in love with the place known as “the city of love”. It’s a perfect destination for everybody, especially those who are in love!

Hunting and fishing tours, water skiing

Lopota Lake Resort & Spa is the best place for various outdoor activities. Enjoy the time with your friends and family and try new things!
